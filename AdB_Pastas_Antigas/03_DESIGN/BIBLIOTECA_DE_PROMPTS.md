# COMO USAR ESTA BIBLIOTECA

Este documento não contém prompts completos.

Contém blocos reutilizáveis que podem ser combinados para construir qualquer prompt necessário.

## Estrutura de um prompt completo

```
[BLOCO DE AMBIENTE] + [BLOCO DE ILUMINAÇÃO] + [BLOCO DE ENQUADRAMENTO]
+ [BLOCO DE SUJEITO] + [BLOCO DE EXPRESSÃO] + [BLOCO DE TEXTURA]
+ [BLOCO DE RESTRIÇÃO OBRIGATÓRIA]
```

## Regra de montagem

1. Escolha um bloco de cada seção relevante
2. Cole na ordem sugerida
3. Adicione sempre o BLOCO DE RESTRIÇÃO OBRIGATÓRIA ao final
4. Revise o prompt completo com o Teste Final (6 perguntas do GUIA_DIRECAO_DE_ARTE_AB.md)

# SEÇÃO 1 — TIPOS DE ILUMINAÇÃO

---

## LUZ-01 — Luz natural matinal (janela lateral)

```
Warm morning natural light entering from a side window on the left.
Soft golden quality. No harsh shadows. Even and diffused.
Interior temperature approximately 3000K.
```

---

## LUZ-02 — Luz natural de tarde (janela lateral)

```
Afternoon natural light entering from a side window.
Slightly warmer than morning — golden, inviting.
Long soft shadows. Warm interior atmosphere.
```

---

## LUZ-03 — Luz artificial LED quente (interior noturno ou sem janela)

```
Warm LED interior lighting, color temperature 2700K–3000K.
Overhead diffused light, no harsh spots.
Warm, professional atmosphere. No fluorescent cold tone.
```

---

## LUZ-04 — Luz lateral difusa (estúdio documental)

```
Single diffused light source from the left side, simulating a large window.
Creates soft modeling shadows revealing textures.
Clean, professional — not dramatic.
```

---

## LUZ-05 — Luz ambiente mista (interior com luz natural e artificial)

```
Mixed light: natural light from window on left, warm fill LED on right.
Balanced, no dominant color cast.
Natural professional interior atmosphere.
```

---

## LUZ-06 — Luz de detalhe (close, macro)

```
Raking side light — light source at approximately 45 degrees to surface.
Reveals texture of materials: wood grain, leather, metal finish.
Soft and precise. Minimal fill from opposite side.
```

---

## LUZ-07 — Luz flat (infográficos e flat lay)

```
Even, flat overhead lighting. No shadows.
Clean, graphic quality. Works for product and flat lay photography.
Neutral to slightly warm tone.
```

# SEÇÃO 2 — ENQUADRAMENTOS E ÂNGULOS

---

## ENCUADRE-01 — Close / Macro

```
Tight close-up shot.
Subject fills approximately 70–80% of the frame.
Background nearly invisible or very softly blurred.
Used for: tool details, texture, finishing results.
```

---

## ENCUADRE-02 — Plano médio

```
Medium shot from approximately waist or chest height.
Subject centered or slightly off-center.
Background partially visible and contextual.
Used for: barbeiro em ação, interação barbeiro-cliente.
```

---

## ENCUADRE-03 — Plano médio aberto

```
Mid-wide shot showing subject from head to approximately knee level.
Background clearly visible and relevant to scene.
Used for: full barber station, barbeiro de pé trabalhando.
```

---

## ENCUADRE-04 — Plano geral / Ambiente

```
Wide shot of the entire or most of the space.
All key elements of the environment visible.
People secondary to the space, if present.
Used for: interior do ambiente, fachadas, antes/depois de espaço.
```

---

## ENCUADRE-05 — Flat lay (overhead)

```
Directly overhead, camera pointing straight down.
Subject arranged on a flat surface.
No perspective distortion. Geometric and clean.
Used for: ferramentas, materiais impressos, produtos.
```

---

## ENCUADRE-06 — Ângulo levemente superior (3/4)

```
Camera positioned slightly above eye level, angled down at approximately 15–20 degrees.
Natural, documentary feel.
Used for: ambiente geral, client seated in chair.
```

---

## ENCUADRE-07 — Perfil (lateral)

```
Subject photographed from the side, 90 degrees to camera.
Used for: antes/depois de corte (always paired for consistency), barbeiro applying razor.
```

---

## ENCUADRE-08 — 3/4 frontal

```
Subject at approximately 45 degrees to camera — between frontal and profile.
Most common portrait angle.
Used for: retratos de clientes, barbeiros, expressões.
```

---

## ENCUADRE-09 — Reflexo no espelho

```
Shot taken looking into a mirror.
Main subject is the reflection — creates depth and context.
Camera visible in reflection should be minimized or obscured.
Used for: cliente avaliando resultado, interação barbeiro-cliente no espelho.
```

Head tilted slightly to one side.

## EXPR-BAR-04 — Neutro, executando técnica

Barber expression: neutral and skilled.
No strong emotion. Simply working.
Hands visible and active.
Professional competence without performance.

# SEÇÃO 4 — EXPRESSÕES DE CLIENTES

---

## EXPR-CLI-01 — Relaxado na cadeira

```
Client expression: relaxed and comfortable.
Eyes closed or looking down softly.
Shoulders visibly at ease.
No tension in jaw or brow.
Trust communicated through body language.
```

---

## EXPR-CLI-02 — Avaliando resultado no espelho

Client expression: genuine assessment.
Looking at own reflection in the mirror.
Expression between neutral and quietly satisfied.
Not an exaggerated smile — realistic.

---

## EXPR-CLI-03 — Conversando com barbeiro

Client expression: natural conversation.
Looking at barber in mirror or turning slightly to speak.
Engaged but relaxed. Not performing.
Mouth slightly open or closed depending on moment captured.
```

---

## EXPR-CLI-04 — Chegando / sendo recebido

```
Client expression: casual entry recognition.
Light smile of recognition or simple neutral acknowledgment.
Body language: relaxed, familiar — this is a regular place.
Not formal. Not performative.

---

## EXPR-CLI-05 — Criança, leve tensão natural

```
Child client expression: slightly nervous but cooperative.
Eyes forward, small hands gripping the cape slightly.

# SEÇÃO 5 — TEXTURAS E MATERIAIS

---

## TEXT-01 — Madeira natural

```
Natural wood surface with visible grain.
Tone: warm brown, medium to light.
Finish: matte or lightly oiled — not lacquered or shiny.
Texture clearly visible. Authentic, not synthetic.
```

---

## TEXT-02 — Metal fosco (preto ou escuro)

```
Matte black or dark metal surface.
No high shine. Subtle light reflection only.
Used for: tool holders, chair hardware, shelf brackets.
Professional and understated.
```

---

## TEXT-03 — Metal brilhante (inox discreto)

```
Stainless steel or chrome-finished metal.
Controlled reflection — catches light naturally.
Not excessive shine or over-polish.
Used for: scissors blades, clipper body, small hardware.
```

---

## TEXT-04 — Couro (assentos, navalha)

```
Genuine or high-quality faux leather surface.
Dark brown, black, or oxblood tone.
Texture: slightly worn, authentic. Not brand new or stiff.
Used for: chair seat, strop leather for razor.
```

---

## TEXT-05 — Tecido (avental, capa, toalha)

```
Cotton or poly-blend fabric surface.
Colors: black, white, dark gray, navy.
Texture: clean, slightly wrinkled from use — authentic.
Not ironed to perfection. Professionally clean.
```

---

## TEXT-06 — Vidro (espelho, pote)

```
Clean glass surface.
Mirror: reflective, minimal smudging.
Glass jar or container: transparent, clean, with visible content.
Used for: espelhos, potes de produto, organizadores.
```

---

## TEXT-07 — Cimento / concreto liso

```
Smooth concrete or cement-look surface.
Light gray tone. Minimal imperfections.
Used for: fundos neutros, paredes de apoio.
No decorative patterns.
```

---

## TEXT-08 — Cerâmica simples

```
Ceramic floor or wall tile — simple, residential or commercial grade.
Colors: white, off-white, beige, light gray.
Grout lines visible. Clean and maintained.
Not decorative patterns. Not marble-look luxury ceramic.
```

---

## TEXT-09 — Papel / impresso

```
Printed paper surface — guide, checklist, or workbook page.
Slightly warm white paper tone.
Text and graphic elements partially visible but not legible.
Natural page curvature if open book.
```

```
Male client, 18–25 years old, Brazilian appearance.
Dressed casually: sneakers, casual pants, t-shirt or hoodie.
Phone visible in pocket or hand.
Skin tone: varied — light to dark.
Expression: casual and relaxed.
```

## CLI-03 — Pai de família (35–50 anos)

```
Male client, 35–50 years old. Visibly a family man.
Dressed: casual weekend wear — polo shirt or casual button-down.
Wedding ring visible or implied.
Expression: patient and familiar with the routine.
Build: average adult. No model physique.

# SEÇÃO 8 — ESTILOS DE COMPOSIÇÃO

---

## COMP-01 — Composição respirada (muito espaço negativo)

```
Composition with intentional negative space — at least 40% of frame is empty.
Subject placed in rule-of-thirds intersection.
No visual clutter.
Used when image will receive text overlay.
```

---

## COMP-02 — Composição centrada (simetria intencional)

```
Subject centered in frame with symmetrical balance.
Used for: espelhos frontais, fachadas, before/after.
Clean and ordered composition.
```

---

## COMP-03 — Composição diagonal (dinamismo sutil)

```
Main subject or leading line follows a diagonal from lower-left to upper-right (or reverse).
Adds energy without chaos.
Used for: barbeiro em ação, mãos em movimento.
```

---

## COMP-04 — Composição de camadas (foreground + subject + background)

```
Three visible planes: foreground element slightly out of focus, sharp subject in middle, background contextual.
Creates depth and authenticity.
Used for: cenas cotidianas, ambientes com pessoas.
```

---

## COMP-05 — Flat lay organizado

```
Overhead shot with items arranged with intentional spacing.
Items: not touching each other — breathing room between each object.
All items aligned to an invisible grid.
Used for: ferramentas, produtos, materiais impressos.
```

# SEÇÃO 9 — FUNDOS E CENÁRIOS

---

## FUNDO-01 — Parede simples off-white

```
Background: off-white or warm white painted wall.
Slight texture visible — not perfectly smooth.
No decorations or patterns.
Warm, neutral. Works for portraits and detail shots.
```

---

## FUNDO-02 — Interior de barbearia desfocado

```
Background: out-of-focus barbershop interior.
Colors visible: warm tones, wood, organized products on shelf.
No specific detail legible — just contextual atmosphere.
Used for: retratos, closes com contexto.
```

---

## FUNDO-03 — Fachada da rua

```
Background: Brazilian street or sidewalk.
Simple commercial or residential neighborhood.
Not wealthy district, not favela — regular middle neighborhood.
Time of day: daytime, overcast or golden hour.
```

---

## FUNDO-04 — Superfície de madeira (flat lay)

```
Background: clean wooden surface, natural grain.
Tone: medium to light brown.
No strong marks or scratches — clean work surface.
Used for: mockups, flat lay de ferramentas.
```

---

## FUNDO-05 — Superfície de concreto claro

```
Background: smooth light concrete or cement surface.
Light gray, minimal texture.
No strong markings.
Used for: mockups de materiais, flat lay neutro.
```

---

## FUNDO-06 — Espelho (reflexo como fundo)

```
Background: mirror reflection showing barbershop interior.
Creates depth — the background IS the context in reflected form.
Used for: cenas clássicas de barbearia.
```

---

## FUNDO-07 — Escuro neutro (produto em destaque)

```
Background: very dark — near black or very dark brown.
Used only for product-focus images where maximum contrast is needed.
Not for portraits or environmental shots.
```

# SEÇÃO 10 — PERSPECTIVAS

---

## PERSP-01 — Altura dos olhos (natural)

```
Camera at natural standing eye level — approximately 165–175cm.
Creates equal, respectful relationship with subject.
Most natural and relatable perspective.
```

---

## PERSP-02 — Altura dos olhos do cliente sentado

```
Camera at seated person's eye level — approximately 100–115cm.
Creates intimacy, equality.
Used when shooting the client's perspective from inside the chair.
```

---

## PERSP-03 — Levemente superior (3/4 overhead)

```
Camera approximately 20–30 degrees above natural eye level.
Shows environment clearly. Reduces floor, increases ceiling context.
Used for: ambientes, mesas organizadas, espera.
```

---

## PERSP-04 — Levemente inferior (empoderamento)

```
Camera approximately 15–20 degrees below natural eye level.
Creates sense of confidence and presence in the subject.
Used carefully: barbeiro autoridade, resultado de corte impressionante.
Not exaggerated — documentary, not cinematic hero shot.
```

---

## PERSP-05 — Overhead direto (90 graus)

```
Camera directly above subject, pointing straight down.
Perfect for flat lay.
No perspective — objects appear in true proportion.
```

# BLOCO DE RESTRIÇÃO OBRIGATÓRIA

**Adicionar SEMPRE ao final de qualquer prompt:**

```
Documentary-style photography. Realistic and authentic. No excessive post-processing.
No luxury elements. No unattainable scenarios. Brazilian neighborhood context.
Real people with character — not advertising models.
Warm, natural lighting. Organized and clean but simple environment.
The image should make a Brazilian neighborhood barber think: "I can do this."
```

```
LUZ-03 + ENCUADRE-09 + CLI-01 + EXPR-CLI-02 + TEXT-06 (espelho) + FUNDO-06 + RESTRIÇÃO OBRIGATÓRIA

## Combinação C — Flat lay de ferramentas

```
LUZ-07 + ENCUADRE-05 + TEXT-03 (metal) + TEXT-01 (madeira) + FUNDO-04 + COMP-05 + RESTRIÇÃO OBRIGATÓRIA
```

## Combinação D — Ambiente vazio

```
LUZ-01 + ENCUADRE-04 + FUNDO-01 (paredes) + TEXT-08 (piso) + COMP-02 + RESTRIÇÃO OBRIGATÓRIA
